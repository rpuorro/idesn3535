About VectorOctave

About VectorOctave Developed as an application for a simultaneous auditory and visual composition,VectorOctave is a drawing platform, an instrument, and an experience — meant to be sketched, played, and discovered. As you draw within the art-board, musical “events” are triggered, Just as you would trigger notes to play from a synthesizer when dragging your hand across the keys. So, for example, the faster you are drawing, the faster the musical notes are being played.

How It Works

VectorOctave’s Artboard is made up of a coordinate plane, which has been populated with an array of musical chords, notes, electronic sound samples, percussion, and ambient sound effects. The sounds have been pre-loaded into the art board and programmed to play as soon as your stylus or mouse “hits” that particular point on the art board where the sound is located.

Getting Started

Color

First, click on any of the colored circles within toolbar to select brush color. Switching between colors frequently throughout your composition will lead to a more dynamic experience.

Loops

Next, choose a percussion loop. 87BPM is a good place to start

Saving

When finished, click the icon to save, or the icon to clear the artboard and start again.
